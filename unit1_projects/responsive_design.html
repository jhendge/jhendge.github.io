How to Survive a Trip to Ikea

Paradise!

Have you ever been to college? Ever furnished an apartment on a tight budget? Ever felt the burning to desire to munch on Swedish meatballs at 11:30 in the morning? Then you've been to that wonderful blue and yellow box of fun, Ikea! That's right boys and girls, the amusement park known as Ikea has been serving up self-assembly furniture (checkout their awesome products!) and dishing out Swedish meatballs in their cafeterias since 1943 (ok, I'm not actually sure they had cafeterias back in the 40s...)!

While Ikea is known for its low cost, stylish furniture, it has also gained the reputation as the most difficult store to get out of. Between its signature curvy path, monstrous cafeteria, and eye-popping deals at every corner, a trip to Ikea could last up to seven or eight hours! That's right... seven or eight hours! In light of that, I have put together a few helpful tips that should make your next trip to Ikea more efficient!

  1. Know what you're going to buy and don't stray from your list
  2. Walk around the curvy path as quickly as possible
  3. Stuff your pockets with meatballs - you'll need them for energy
  4. Focus!
  5. Leave...just leave
*Remember, never assemble Ikea furniture alone.. always be sure to have a power drill and a beer at your side! Not sure what beer is, read this!*
